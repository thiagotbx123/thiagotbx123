### 👋 About

I'm a **Solutions Architect at TestBox (remote, San Francisco)**, leading the largest customer account. My work sits where applied AI meets real implementation: I design synthetic-data pipelines, build multi-agent automation, and ship Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers that production teams actually use.

Before SaaS I spent 10+ years in project delivery and data at **Vale, Rede D'Or and Nestlé**, so I bring the business lens, not just the code.

- 🤖 **Generative AI in production:** synthetic datasets at scale (**194k+ records, 74 tables, 8 multi-entity companies**) with validated referential integrity, built with Claude, Gemini and OpenAI.
- 🔌 **MCP &amp; integrations:** OAuth 2.0 MCP servers for accounting platforms, plus multi-agent flows for data validation and knowledge extraction.
- 🎭 **Test automation:** a Playwright validation engine covering **29 product features** with evidence capture, screen scoring and Excel reporting.
- 🧩 **Multi-agent systems:** contributed to an autonomous platform (FastAPI, React, Next.js, Weaviate) with REST &amp; WebSocket orchestration across hundreds of endpoints.
